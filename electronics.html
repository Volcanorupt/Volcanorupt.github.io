IR-Receiver Receives infrared signals sent by infrared remotes with a 4-channel IR switch, 4x4 female stud connector at the bottom, 2 peg holes on the front and 2 electrical outputs (red output and blue output based on the controller's colour settings).
IR-Toggle Remote Sends 3-directional infrared commands to the receiver on the corresponding output colour. It has 2 toggle switches, 2 directional inverters and a 4-channel IR switch.
IR-Speed Dial Remote Control Sends scalar-measured infrared commands to the receiver on the corresponding output colour. It has 2 scalar dials, 2 stop buttons, 2 directional inverters and a 4-channel IR switch.
Lights Emanates light from 2 flexible light stalks that fits into peg holes comfortably for nifty fitting. Consumes 9 watts and is considered efficient. The brightness can be controlled with the IR-Speed Dial Remote Control.
