Lego Technic & Power Functions: Higher-End Model Building & Animation

A bulding toy aimed at expert Lego builders aged from 11 to 18. Builders can create real-life architecture and vehicles with interconnecting plastic connectors and beams.

Lego can be very expensive and limited, therfore these toys can be only found in selected retail toy shops like Toys-R-Us and online shops like Lego Education and the official Lego website.

They are normally found in medium sized sets containing around 300 pieces. Power Functions hardly ever come in sets and must be bought individually via online purchases from the 2 Lego stores.

Subthemes of Lego Technic:

  • Mindstorms
  • Racers
  • Bionicle
  • Exo-Force
