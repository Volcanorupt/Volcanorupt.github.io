Vintage Car

Building Difficulty: Difficult

Range of Components Used:

  • Gears
  • Beams
  • Pegs
  • Wheels
  • Axles

Made with 600 pieces. Movable wheels, steering, side mirrors, doors, engine fan. Stablised the car with an additional layer of beam frames. Pimped up the bonnet with an exposed V6 engine for style.




Fire Truck

Building Difficulty: Expert

Range of Components Used:

  • Gears
  • Beams
  • Pegs
  • Wheels
  • Axles
  • Axle Connectors

Made with 1500 pieces. Completely movable liftarm controlled with 2 protruding gears on the sides that could be hidden by the red folding covers. Movable doors, steering, side mirrors ans side mounts to balance the fire truck when doing heavy duty work. The reels at the back could also rotate freely to position the bucket effortlessly back into the boot of the truck. The truch houses a universal turntable that can not only rotate the arm 360 degrees, but the gears can shift to change the height of the expanding arm.


Street Truck

Building Difficulty: Difficult

Range of Components Used:

  • Gears
  • Beams
  • Pegs
  • Wheels
  • Axles
  • Axle Connectors
  • Springs

Made with 950 pieces. Movable wheels, steering and doors. This truck has independent suspension to take on rough terrain without problems, and having big rugged wheels would increase traction.
